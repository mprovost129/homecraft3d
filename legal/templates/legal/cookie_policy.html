Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Home Craft 3D ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Why do we use cookies?

  • To enable essential site functionality
  • To analyze site usage and improve performance
  • To remember your preferences
  • To support security and authentication

Your choices

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, some parts of this website may become inaccessible or not function properly.
